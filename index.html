PORSCHE 911 GT3

A digitally illustrated side-profile artwork of a Porsche sports car, created with a focus on precision, clean linework, and balanced shading. The design highlights form, aerodynamics, and modern automotive aesthetics while maintaining a minimalist composition. Styled typography complements the illustration and completes a refined, poster-like visual piece.
